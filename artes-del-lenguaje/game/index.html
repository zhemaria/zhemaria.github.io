❓ How to Play

🎯 Objective

Sink all 7 enemy ships using Cartesian coordinates.
The ships are visible — practice locating the correct coordinates!

📍 Coordinates

Every cell has a coordinate (X, Y):
· X = column number (horizontal axis, 1 to 10)
· Y = row number (vertical axis, 1 to 10)

Example: ship at column 3, row 5 → fire at (3, 5)

🔥 How to Fire

Option A: Click directly on any board cell.
Option B: Type X and Y in the "Fire Control" panel and press FIRE or Enter.

🚢 Enemy Fleet

🚤Tugboat × 21 cell each
🚢Destroyer × 32 cells each
⛴️Cruiser × 13 cells
🛳️Aircraft Carrier × 14 cells

💥 Shot Results

💥 Hit — you struck a ship!
💧 Miss — nothing there, try another cell.
When all cells of a ship are hit → ship sunk!
